About Us

At Flower Knows, we believe that makeup is more than just a routine—it is a portal to a dreamscape. Founded in 2016, we have dedicated ourselves to merging the ethereal charm of European Rococo elegance with the innovative spirit of C-Beauty. We don't just create cosmetics; we craft artefacts for your vanity. Every collection is an immersive narrative, inviting you to step into a world of celestial angels, enchanted forests, and vintage toy theatres.

Our Vision

Intricate Craftsmanship

Our signature 3D-engraved palettes and custom-moulded lipsticks are the result of rigorous design, ensuring that every product feels like a piece of high jewellery in your hand.

Whimsical Storytelling

From the "Swan Ballet" to "Moonlight Mermaid," we launch themed collections that transform the makeup experience into a seasonal fairytale.

Inclusive Radiance

We curate soft, dream-like colour stories that enhance natural beauty, providing high-pigment payoff with a weightless, ethereal finish.

Our Collection

Bunny Garden

A whimsical, springtime-inspired series that celebrates the charm of an English country garden, showcasing adorable rabbit illustrations and a palette of fresh, pastel florals that evoke a sunny afternoon tea party.

Strawberry Rococo

A sweet, vintage-inspired collection that blends 18th-century French elegance with charming strawberry and cherub motifs.

The Sweetie Bear

This collection captures the cosy warmth of a childhood bedroom with its 3D-embossed teddy bear motifs, featuring soft, "milk tea" neutral tones and matte finishes that feel as comforting as a plush toy.
