About Us

Where Beauty Meets Fantasy

At Flower Knows, we believe that makeup is more than just a routine — it is a portal to a dreamscape. Founded in 2016, we have dedicated ourselves to merging the ethereal charm of European Rococo elegance with the innovative spirit of C-Beauty.

We don't just create cosmetics; we craft artifacts for your vanity. Every collection is an immersive narrative, inviting you to step into a world of celestial angels, enchanted forests, and vintage toy theaters.

How long will it take to get my order? ▼
It depends on where you are. Overseas deliveries can take anywhere from 6-15 business days. Delivery details will be provided in your confirmation email.
What should I do if my product has a problem? ▼
Please contact us for compensation within 15 days after the package status shows as delivered. Include your order number, the name of the product(s), the reason for the claim, and any photos of the product(s).
